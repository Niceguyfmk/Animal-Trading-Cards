Lion

Lions are symbols of strength and courage and have been celebrated throughout history for these characteristics.

  • Scientific Name: Panthera leo
  • Average Length: 6 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 12 years
  • Habitat: Grasslands

Male lions are easy to recognize thanks to their distinctive manes. African lions eat large animals that they find in the grasslands, including antelopes, zebras and wildebeest. They work as a group and use intelligent hunting tactics to catch prey which they would not be able to catch alone as they are faster than them.
